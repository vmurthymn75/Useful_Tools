About This Unit Converter

Our free universal unit converter provides precise conversions across multiple measurement categories including length, weight, volume, temperature, speed, and area. Whether you're working on a project, cooking, traveling, or studying, our tool makes unit conversion simple and accurate.

Supported Conversion Categories

  • Length: Convert between millimeters, centimeters, meters, kilometers, inches, feet, yards, miles
  • Weight: Convert between milligrams, grams, kilograms, metric tons, ounces, pounds, stones
  • Volume: Convert between milliliters, liters, cubic meters, cubic inches, cubic feet, gallons, quarts, pints, cups
  • Temperature: Convert between Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin
  • Speed: Convert between meters/second, kilometers/hour, miles/hour, knots, feet/second
  • Area: Convert between square millimeters, square centimeters, square meters, hectares, square kilometers, square inches, square feet, square yards, acres, square miles

How to Use

  1. Select the measurement category (length, weight, etc.)
  2. Enter the amount you want to convert
  3. Select your "from" unit (the unit you're converting from)
  4. Select your "to" unit (the unit you're converting to)
  5. Click "Convert" to see the result
  6. Use the swap button to quickly reverse the units

Common Conversions

  • Length: Inches to centimeters, feet to meters, miles to kilometers
  • Weight: Pounds to kilograms, ounces to grams
  • Volume: Cups to milliliters, gallons to liters
  • Temperature: Fahrenheit to Celsius, Celsius to Kelvin
  • Speed: Miles per hour to kilometers per hour
